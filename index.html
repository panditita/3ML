3ML works like this:

First your pupils read a very short story, chosen - by you or them - from over 1000 in our library. Then they complete four activities to help them understand and remember what they've read.

If you haven't used 3ml before with your pupils, please take a look at the short videos on our Guidance page.

Sign up and try it out!

It is free to all SSERC member schools.
